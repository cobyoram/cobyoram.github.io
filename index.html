Intro

I am a data scientist skilled in creating actionable insights using machine learning algorithms and statistical modeling.
A leader at the top of my data science cohort, I am proficient in multiple programming languages. I excel at creating business insights from raw data, from cleaning and feature engineering to evaluating supervised and unsupervised learning techniques. I have developed a particular interest in specialized fields like automation, language processing, and deep learning. I am excited to keep learning, as data science is a growing industry that renders obsolete anyone who believes they've learned it all. Check out my work.

About

Besides being a Data Science, I am a performer and an entrepreneur.
In my employment history, I have roamed several disciplines to find my passion. While I gained a lot of experience in fields like sales, customer service, and web design, the computer science industry captivated my attention. From a natural aptitude to conceptualize object-oriented scripts to the intensely satisfying challenge of debugging, programming wholly appeals to my skill-set and character. Data science in particular, with its blend of mathematical reasoning, business understanding, and programming savvy, constitutes my professional pursuits.
